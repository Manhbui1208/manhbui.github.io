## About Me

I'm currently a research associate at the BioRAI Lab, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), working with [Asst. Prof. Lin Wang](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=SReb2csAAAAJ&hl=en). Previously, I received my M.Sc. research degree from the University of Technology Sydney (UTS), Australia, in early 2025, where I worked under the supervision of [Assoc. Prof. Hoang Dinh](https://sites.google.com/view/dinh-thai-hoang/) and [Assoc. Prof. Diep Nguyen](https://sites.google.com/view/diep-n-nguyen/). Before that, I obtained my B.Sc. degree in electronics and communication engineering from VNU-UET in 2023.

- **Machine Learning:** meta-learning, incremental learning, transfer learning
